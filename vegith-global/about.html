About Vegith Global

Built on trust. Engineered for operational excellence.

An integrated organization strengthened by MLPL — bringing together vendor flexibility and owned fleet reliability through SOPs, centralized monitoring and trained operations teams.

Our Story

A logistics enterprise that grew with India's industries.

Vegith Global Services Limited began with one conviction — that disciplined execution and centralized visibility could transform how Indian enterprises move goods. Today, Vegith operates as an integrated organization combining a verified vendor fleet network with the owned reliability of MLPL, its dedicated fleet subsidiary.

Across industries — manufacturing, steel, FMCG, automotive, retail and 3PL — Vegith is recognized for trip discipline, on-ground execution and an unwavering commitment to operational excellence.

Company Philosophy

Reliability is non-negotiable. Visibility is the standard. Excellence is the habit.

  • Trip discipline over volume chasing
  • Owned + vendor hybrid for resilience
  • Centralized command, decentralized execution
  • Long-term partnerships over short-term wins

Our Mission

To create seamless, technology-driven logistics that inspire trust, elevate operational excellence and deliver customer delight — while building an ecosystem rooted in integrity, innovation and respect.

Our Vision

To lead India with innovative smart fleet management technology and become the nation's most trusted integrated facility and manpower services partner.
