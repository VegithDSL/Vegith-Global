Who We Are

An integrated logistics partner built for scale, trust and visibility.

Vegith Global Services Limited is an integrated logistics partner offering reliable transportation, seamless coordination and end-to-end operational support for businesses across India. The company combines the agility of a strong vendor fleet network with the dependability of an owned fleet operated through MLPL, its dedicated fleet subsidiary.

With centralized trip monitoring, disciplined execution and commitment to on-time delivery, Vegith ensures smooth movement of goods across industries and geographies. Founded and led by Kailash Sharma, Vegith stands on trust, operational excellence and long-term partnerships.

Vegith HR functions as a specialized company offering skilled manpower for warehouses, fulfillment centers and office operations. Together Vegith Global, MLPL and Vegith HR deliver scalable logistics and workforce solutions backed by experience, integrity and consistent performance.
